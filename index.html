About

I hold the position of Chief Technical Officer at Zamineh Art Platform, which introduces emerging artists to the market. My career at Zamineh begins with curating The Art Genome Project for the local and primary Iran Art Scene. Furthermore, I am a fellow at the Center for Complex Networks and Social Data (CCNSD) at Shahid Beheshti University (SBU). The Twitter Project holds workshops and academic research in Social Science and Complex Networks at CCNSD.

I completed my master's degree in SBU at Complex Systems and Non-linear Dynamics, where Dr. G. R. Jafari advised me. With the consideration of complex networks and critical phenomena, my thesis focuses on Social Data and some trends in Data Science. This is based on Iranian activities in the cyber and public sphere, such as Twitter. My thesis title for my master's degree was on the Station and Detection Bot of Social Media.
